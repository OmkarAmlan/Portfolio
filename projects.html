Projects and Hackathons!

GeeksForGeeks - Solving For India Hackathon

e-GymBro - Devised a web-based application to aid and assisst beginner lifters on their fitness journey by implementing image processing libraries like MediaPipe, GCP products and 3D trigonometry to suggest posture corrections during workouts. This web-app also contains personalised dashboards, progress tracker charts and ML model to predict body fat percentages based on body measurements!

Data Dive! - My Dive into ML and Data Science

MLtiVerse - Participated in MUJ's 36-hour Machine Learning hackathon and ranked 12th, where given data and stats of football players, I devised an appropriate model to predict the values of players. The model was implemented after careful data exploration and via a Random Forest Regressor.

All In Strategy Challenge - As part of IIT Delhi's Strategy Challenge, I ran sentimental analysis on reviews for the PokerBaazi app, to obtain possible problems and recommend solutions.
